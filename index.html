Tata Motors PV Segment — Industry Research Report

Over the past few weeks, I worked on an in-depth analysis of the Indian Passenger Vehicle industry, with a focus on Tata Motors' transformation story.

What started as a simple research exercise turned into a deep dive into the SUV-led structural shift in India, the rise of EVs and Tata Motors' leadership, changing consumer behaviour and purchase drivers, and competitive dynamics across Maruti, Hyundai, Mahindra, and emerging EV players.

This report combines industry data with primary research and strategic frameworks like SWOT, Porter's Five Forces, and PESTLE to understand where the market is heading.
